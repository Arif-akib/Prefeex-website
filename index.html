    About Us

    Prefeex Limited is a rising conglomerate promoting digital transformation of lifestyle. We have multiple ventures designed to meet your needs in business, work and everyday life. Through our innovative solutions we plan to digitalise all walks of life. Our innovations are designed to bring easy solutions and a comfortable lifestyle through digital transformation. Our expertise expands far and wide as we work closely to improve the lives of industry workers and industry owners. We provide a variety of services including worker welfare, product supply and corporate brand promotion.Starting in 2019, Prefeex Limited is successfully running its operation in three different sectors that include Bhorosha store, Pmart and Prefly Digital. Bhorosha Store is a welfare initiative for the industry workers to bring ease and comfort to their lifestyle. Pmart and Prefly Digital on the other hand, are providing dedicated services for the corporate sector to ensure total reliability towards its customers.
